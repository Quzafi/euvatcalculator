Our Story

EU VAT Calculator was created to solve a common problem: finding accurate, up-to-date VAT rates for different European countries. As a team of European business professionals, we experienced firsthand how complicated VAT calculations can be when dealing with multiple EU countries.

We launched in 2024 with a simple goal: create the most accurate and easy-to-use VAT calculator for the European market. Today, thousands of users across Europe rely on our tool for their VAT calculations.
